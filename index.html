bio

Howdy! I'm a computer security research scientist with the Astrolavos Lab. Previously, I received my Ph.D. under the supervision of Wenke Lee and Manos Antonakakis and I work in the areas of adversarial and applied machine learning, threat attribution, and network security. My interests are broad and I've also done work in botnet takedowns, mobile device security, dynamic malware analysis, web security, and information retrieval. I received my undergrad from IIT where I was a member of the IIT (now Georgetown) Information Retrieval Lab led by Ophir Frieder. My stint in web security was alongside Dawn Song's WebBlaze research group.
