About Me

Who Am I:

My name is Yacin Nadji and I'm a Junior in the Computer Science department at the Illinois Institute of Technology located in Chicago, IL. I made this website for several reasons. The first being I wanted to teach myself XHTML/CSS. I do not intend to make a career out of web design, but a basic amount of knowledge in the field is something everyone in Computer Science ought to have. Secondly, I want an easy way to show friends, family and potential employers who I am, what I do, and what I have accomplished. Naturally, as a CS major, technology is my medium.

I am a member of Chicago's Linux User Group and I work at the Information Retrieval Lab as a Research Assistant, working on a variety of projects.

What I Hope To Accomplish:

First things first, I'd like to get my undergraduate degree taken care of. Originally, I was planning on getting my B.S., finding work, and continuing on from there. Through the research experiences I've had, I've decided I'd like to continue on with my education and get my PhD in Computer Science. As for specialization is concerned, I haven't quite decided, but I'm a fan of Programming Language Theory, Information Retrieval and Bioinformatics. So we'll see how things turn out.
