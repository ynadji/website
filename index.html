bio

Howdy! I'm a computer security post doctoral researcher with the Astrolavos Lab. Previously, I received my Ph.D. under the supervision of Wenke Lee and Manos Antonakakis and I work in the areas of botnet takedowns, DNS security and applied machine learning. My interests are broad and I've also done work in mobile device security, dynamic malware analysis, web security, and information retrieval. I received my undergrad from IIT where I was a member of the IIT (now Georgetown) Information Retrieval Lab led by Ophir Frieder. My stint in web security was alongside Dawn Song's WebBlaze research group.
