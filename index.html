bio

Howdy! I'm a computer security Ph.D. candidate at Georgia Tech. My advisor is Wenke Lee and I work in the areas of network-level threat attribution, DNS security and applied machine learning. My interests are broad and I've also done work in mobile device security, dynamic malware analysis, web security, and information retrieval. I received my undergrad from IIT where I was a member of the IIT Information Retrieval Lab led by Ophir Frieder. My stint in web security was alongside Dawn Song's WebBlaze research group.
